RFP Details - RFP-001

Project Overview

Trade: Site Preparation

Project: 24-Unit Multi-Family Building

Location: TBD (urban setting, assume 0.5-acre lot)

Area: Approx. 21,780 sq ft (0.5 acres)

Scope of Work

Description: Clear and prepare site for construction, including vegetation removal, excavation for foundation, and grading for drainage.

Tasks:

  • Clear vegetation and debris
  • Excavate for foundation (150 cubic yards)
  • Grade site for positive drainage
  • Install temporary erosion control measures

Benefit

Ensuring a properly prepared site to support the timely start of foundation work and overall project schedule.

Objective

To deliver a cleared, graded, and regulation-compliant site ready for construction within the specified timeline.

Value

Reducing project delays and ensuring compliance with local zoning and environmental standards, minimizing risks and costs.
